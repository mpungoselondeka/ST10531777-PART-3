lUHLELO ORGANISATION
Description:
Water is a basic need, we need it in our everyday life, be it for drinking, hygiene, cooking, or agriculture, but in South Africa we have a water crisis. our people rely on water provided by the government through local municipalities, but they face water scarcity everyday and their problems take long to get attended to simply because of a lack of means to quickly communicate with their local municipality.

The Luhlelo organisation website has been designed to help communities reach municipalities faster by developing a real time sensor integration whereby the manual system will be bypassed and update the municipal dashboard immediately. We will also have Gis mapping to track all the infrastructure to help keep track and be able to fix them within a short space of time. 

It is made for the people, we have a complaints page where you can lodge a complaint, it has a button where you select the type of issue you are facing, you can also rate its priority and submit photos of evidence.

reasons for choosing this organisation.
safe and clean water for everyone, we are helping people with one of their most basic needs while increasing their lifespan by improving their way of living. Our organisation intends to improve the water quality received by everyone which will help decrease the contracting of water borne diseases like cholera. Although it is mostly catering to the communities it is also of note that it will be also helping municipalities which helps the Government bring good service delivery to the citizens of the country. It’s safe and reliable and there are no costs required from the citizens.
 


Website goals and objectives:
•	Our website is made to enhance transparency of the municipalities by providing the details of the project that they are doing.
•	It’s designed to facilitate communication between the municipalities and communities.
•	To help communities manage to hold the municipalities accountable we also have a complaints page reporting directly to water works department.
•	We also plan to help provide water even in impoverished areas through projects that are funded and through donations that we get.

Current website analysis
Strengths of the existing website
•	Contains basic contact information 
•	It includes online donation button
•	It contains information on the impact our foundation has had on the communities.
•	Has a functioning contact form for enquiries.

weaknesses and areas for improvement
•	Poor mobile responsiveness, the website is not optimised for smartphone or tablets.
•	Absence of a blog or content marketing section to drive organic traffic.
•	No search engine optimisation (SEO) strategy in place.



Key performance indicators
Operational efficiency (through real time sensor reporting)
•	Resolution time: The average time from report to repair
•	Completion rate: The percentage of total reports successfully resolved
•	Cost savings: Estimated money saved by catching leaks early 
Community trust
•	User adoption: Growth in the number of registered citizens over time
•	Gis mapping: mapping where reports come from to ensure all neighbourhoods are being heard equally
Platform health
•	Mobile usage: looking at the percentage of reports made via smartphones-vital for on-the-spot reporting
•	Bounce rate- checking the difficulty of the form by looking at the percentage of people who leave the site without finishing a report.
