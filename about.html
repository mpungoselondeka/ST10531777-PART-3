About us

The Luhlelo organisation is a non-profit organisation designed to help solve the water crisis in South Africa. Its purpose is to help every South African citizen have access to safe and clean water. It acts as a platform where citizens can formally lodge their complaints, and it acts on their behalf to get in contact with municipalities. Municipalities also use this platform to monitor water problems in communities and use GIS mapping to be proactive in maintenance.

Mission

To help communities access clean and safe water and teach people how to protect and conserve water.

Vision

Accessible and clean water for every community.
